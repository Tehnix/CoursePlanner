Set up CoursePlanner

Type in your courses, separated by comma, and the CoursePlanner will use it to categorize
the courses (remember to save it!).

Just copy paste the numbers, including commas, into the input boxes at the top.

Your own courses can be found on the DTU website, usually under your bachelor and either the
study handbook or the bachelor courseplan.
